x.Maximum Subarray (Kadane's Algorithm)

In the Maximum Subarray problem, we have an array of numbers. These numbers can represent anything that increases or decreases over time, such as:
  • Daily profit or loss in a business
  • Daily mood score in a wellness app
  • Daily marks improvement while preparing for exams
  • Daily performance score in a game
  • Daily steps gained or lost compared to target
The main goal is to find a continuous part of this array (called a subarray) that gives the maximum total sum. This helps us find:
  • The best performance streak
  • The most profitable days in a row
  • The highest improvement period
  • The best health progress streak
  • The strongest winning streak in games
